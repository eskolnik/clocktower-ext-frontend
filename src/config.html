Detailed setup instructions here.

Generate and save a Secret Key. You can change this at any time, but you must update it in the browser extension as well.

Drag this bookmark:

BoTC Extension

to your bookmarks bar. Click that bookmark while on the clocktower app to load the extension helper. To configure it, click the "CC" icon in the top right, paste in your Secret Key, and click Save. Then, click the checkbox to allow the extension to start watching your grimoire.

To position the overlay, take a screenshot your stream layout and upload it. Be sure to capture only the 16:9 Twitch player, or the overlay will be misaligned. Use the green controls to upload your screenshot and match the number of players to the number of green circles. Then, use the purple controls to position the circles to match your screenshot.
