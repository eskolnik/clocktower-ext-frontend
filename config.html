Make sure you scroll down to follow all the steps below.

To position the overlay, screenshot your stream layout and upload it. Be sure to capture only the 16:9 Twitch player, or the overlay will be misaligned. Purple settings affect the viewer, green settings are only to help you adjust the overlay.

Generate and save a Secret Key. You can change this at any time, but you must update it in the browser extension as well.

Drag this: BoTC Extension to your bookmarks bar. Click that bookmark while on the clocktower app to load the extension helper. To configure it, click the Twitch icon in the top right, paste in your Secret Key, and click Save. Then, click the checkbox to allow the extension to start watching your grimoire.
